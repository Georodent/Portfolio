MUSIC

George has been surrounded with music from a very young age. His mother and father would play music all the time, and his mother would lock herself in a room and sing to records for hours. He started playing music when he was about 13 with his older brother in metal bands. He began playing drums. Later he picked up guitar, and whatever instument he coould find to make music with. He has played in numerous bands since beggining drums with his older brother. Below are the bands he has played in, and what his role was.

NO ONE: Solo experimental music project. Plays all instruments and does all vox. Guitars, bass, drums, programming, sampling, etc.

No One

THE MILL: Two piece industrial/grind/experimental band. Plays guitar, keys, drum programmming, and vox. Mike Malefactor played bass and vox.

The Mill

GOLY GRIM: Industrial, doom, sludgy, experimental band consisting of guitar, bass, drums, keys, and drum machine. George played guitar and vox. Sandy Roden played bass, noise guitar, drums, sampling and loops. Dale Atkinson played keys, drums, percussions, and various other noise making devices.

Goly Grim

MICROSCOPIC SUFFERING: Current band still involved in. Experimental noise, dark harsh heaviness. Plays various home made and found noise makers, guitar, drums, horns, sampling, keys, and vox. Sandy Roden plays bass, keys, sampling and loops, and vox. Various musicians step in at times.
